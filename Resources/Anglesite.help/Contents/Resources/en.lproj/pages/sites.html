Open and create sites

The Sites window is your starting point — it lists every site you've added so you can jump straight in.

The Sites window

When you open Anglesite, a window titled "Sites" appears. It lists all your sites and is your home base for getting to them. Click any site to open it in its own window.

Opening a site

Click a site in the Sites window to open it. Each site opens in its own window showing a live preview and a chat panel. Anglesite automatically reopens your most-recently-used site the next time you launch the app, so you can pick up right where you left off.

Switching between sites

Every site has its own window. To switch between open sites, use the Window menu or press ⌘` to cycle through open windows.

Creating a new site

To create a new site, use the /anglesite:start command in Claude Code. Once set up, the site folder appears in the Sites window and you can open it from there.
