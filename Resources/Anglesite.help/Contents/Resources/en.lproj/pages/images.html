Adding and optimizing images

Drop an image onto the live preview to replace a picture on your site — Anglesite handles the rest automatically.

Drag an image onto the page

  1. Open the site window and navigate to the page that has the image you want to replace.
  2. Drag an image file from Finder and drop it onto the picture in the live preview.

Anglesite optimizes it automatically

Once you drop the image, Anglesite processes it without any extra steps on your part:

  • Converts the image to WebP for efficient web delivery.
  • Strips embedded metadata (EXIF) to protect privacy and reduce file size.
  • Generates responsive sizes at 480, 768, 1024, and 1920 pixels wide so the image looks sharp on any screen and loads only what's needed.

The optimized images are saved into your site's public/images/ folder. The image they replace is kept safely in public/images/originals/ so nothing is lost. When optimization finishes, the preview updates to show the new image.

If something goes wrong

If an image can't be processed, Anglesite restores the original and shows a message explaining what happened.
