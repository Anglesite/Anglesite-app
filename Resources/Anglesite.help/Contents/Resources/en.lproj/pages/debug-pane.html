Debug pane and logs

The Debug pane streams technical logs from the background processes that power your site preview and builds — useful when something isn't working as expected.

Opening the Debug pane

The Debug pane is hidden by default. To show its menu item, open Settings and turn on Show Debug Pane menu item — this takes effect the next time you open Anglesite. To reveal it just for the current session without changing your settings, hold the ⌥ (Option) key while opening Anglesite.

Once the item is showing, open the pane by choosing View ▸ Show Debug Pane or pressing ⌥⌘D.

What it shows

The Debug pane gives you a live view of the output from all the background processes Anglesite runs while previewing and building your site. It's most useful when something isn't working and you want to see the details, or when you're reporting a problem and need to copy the relevant log lines.
