Updating Anglesite

How you update Anglesite depends on where you got it.

If you downloaded Anglesite directly

Choose Anglesite ▸ Check for Updates… — it appears just below "About Anglesite" in the menu. Anglesite checks for a new version and can install it for you right away.

If you got Anglesite from the Mac App Store

Updates come through the App Store app in the usual way. The in-app "Check for Updates…" menu item is not shown in the App Store version of Anglesite.
