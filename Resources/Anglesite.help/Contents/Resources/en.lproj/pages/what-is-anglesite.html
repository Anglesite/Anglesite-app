What is Anglesite?

Anglesite is a native macOS app that gives your website a home — where you can preview it live, edit it with Claude, check it's ready, and deploy it.

A native home for your website

Anglesite hosts the Anglesite site toolkit. Each site is a folder of files — a self-contained static website — that lives on your Mac. Open the app, pick a site, and you're looking at a live preview alongside a chat window where Claude helps you make changes.

Your files stay yours

The files on your Mac are always the source of truth. You can open your site folder in Finder, edit it in VS Code, or work with it from the Claude Code command line — Anglesite never becomes the only way to get to your content. Everything you create stays right where you put it.

What you can do here

  • Preview your site live — see it running in the app exactly as visitors will see it.
  • Edit with Claude — describe what you want and Claude makes the changes.
  • Check readiness — Anglesite surfaces any issues before you publish.
  • Deploy — send your site live when it's ready.
