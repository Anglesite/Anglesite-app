Health and deploy readiness

A colored dot in the site window toolbar gives you an at-a-glance read on whether your site is ready to publish.

The readiness dot

The dot in the toolbar reflects the same checks that run when you deploy:

  • Green — your site is ready to deploy.
  • Yellow — there are warnings worth reviewing before publishing.
  • Red — there are problems that need to be fixed first.

See what it found

Click the dot to open a popover that lists any problems and warnings. Anglesite builds your site and runs its standard pre-deploy checks, then shows the results here.

Recheck and deploy

After you fix a problem, click Recheck in the popover to run the checks again. The status also refreshes automatically after you deploy.

Ask the assistant for a deeper audit

The popover includes an Ask Assistant button. Clicking it opens the chat and runs a more thorough review of your site so the assistant can suggest improvements or catch issues the automated checks missed.
