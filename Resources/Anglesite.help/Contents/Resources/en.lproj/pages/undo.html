Undoing edits

Every edit Claude makes can be undone from the chat panel — even after you've continued working.

Undo the most recent edit

Each edit row in the chat has an Undo button. Undo is available on the most recent edit you haven't already undone. Click it to reverse that change immediately.

When a file changed outside Anglesite

If the file was modified outside Anglesite — for example in Finder, VS Code, or the command line — since Claude last edited it, Anglesite will pause and ask you to confirm before overwriting your newer changes. You can choose Undo anyway to proceed, or cancel to keep your external changes.

Your history is preserved

Undoing an edit is recorded as a step in your history rather than erasing what came before. Nothing is silently lost — you can always see what was changed and when.
